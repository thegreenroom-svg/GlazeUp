    🖼️ Transfer Pieces — Two-Firing Process

    A transfer cannot go on raw bisque — it needs a glazed, fired surface. These pieces are glaze-fired in Kiln Room above, then the transfer is applied here, then they need a second firing before they're ready for pickup. This takes 10–14 days total, not the usual 5–7 — customers see the longer estimate automatically. Apply each transfer as closely as possible to the customer's saved design — a perfect match can't be guaranteed by hand, and that's clearly disclosed to them upfront.
