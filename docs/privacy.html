What we don't do

  • We don't track your phone. No WiFi tracking, no Bluetooth beacons, no watching you walk in. If you tap "we're here", that's you telling us.
  • We don't sell your data. Not to anyone, ever.
  • We don't advertise at you based on what you paint.
  • We don't build a profile of your family, your diet, or your health from what you write in a note. Staff read it to help you. That's all it's for.

The AI bit, honestly

When your fired pottery comes out of the kiln, it's in a pile with everyone else's. We photograph pieces before firing, and use image matching to work out whose is whose.

Most of the time this happens on our own server and never leaves the building — it's comparing your photo to our photos, mathematically.

When it can't tell, the photo goes to OpenAI's vision service to ask "which of these is it?". It's a photo of a mug. There's no name attached. OpenAI don't keep it or train on it.

Our helper Cleo answers questions the same way. Common questions like "how does the transfer designer work" are answered from a saved answer — no AI, nothing sent anywhere.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can ask us to:

  • Show you everything we hold about you
  • Correct anything wrong
  • Delete it — we'll do it, though we can't post you pottery we no longer have an address for
  • Stop using it for anything you'd rather we didn't

Just ask a member of staff, or email us. No form, no fuss. We'll do it within a month, usually the same day.

If you think we've got it wrong you can complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk. We'd rather you told us first so we can fix it.

Children

Children paint here all the time and we love it. We don't ask children for anything — the booking is in a grown-up's name and that's who we talk to.

If your child collects stickers in Cleo's Club, that's a count of stickers against your booking. Not a profile of your child.
