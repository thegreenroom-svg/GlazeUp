Print as a Transfer
This piece needs two firings
A transfer can only go onto a glazed, fired piece — so after your piece is glaze-fired as normal, staff apply the transfer and it goes through a second firing to set it in place.
Design & print review£1
Second firing£4.50
Total£5.50
📅 Ready in 10–14 days (rather than the usual 5–7) — the extra firing takes real kiln time.
Staff apply your transfer by hand, as closely to your saved design as possible — once printed, it can't be corrected, so do check it looks right first. As with any firing, occasional faults are outside anyone's control.
