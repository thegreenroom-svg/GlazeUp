Chaotic firing schedules

What kilnLINK does

One QR code. Everything your studio needs.

For your customers

Their own studio companion — from the moment they sit down.

Customers scan a QR code at their table and instantly get a personal session app. No download. No account. No friction.

  • Browse all 82 real Mayco glaze colours with their studio codes
  • Photograph their piece and paint colours directly on the photo
  • Sketch ceramic transfers and send to staff for printing
  • Track their pieces through the kiln in real time
  • Share finished work to the community feed or Instagram
  • £5 unlocks all tools on their own phone forever
